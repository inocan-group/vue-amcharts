# Radar Charts

"Radar chart is used to display directional or circular visual representation of a 2-dimensional data.
Think of it as a "circular XY chart", because the two charts are not that different."

Please refer to [amChart's documentation](https://www.amcharts.com/docs/v4/chart-types/radar-chart/) on radar charts for more details.

## How to compose a Radar Chart

A Radar chart consists of the following elements:

- A parent [RadarChart](?path=/docs/documentation-chart-components-radarchart--page) component containing the following in its default slot:
  - At least two axes - radial (that spans from the center of the radar circle) and circular (that goes around the outer perimeter of the radar circle)
  - At least one [RadarSeries](?path=/docs/documentation-chart-components-subcomponents-series-radarseries--page) component.

### Axes

Please refer to the [ValueAxis](?path=/docs/documentation-chart-components-subcomponents-axes-valueaxis--page), [DateAxis](?path=/docs/documentation-chart-components-subcomponents-axes-dateaxis--page) and [CategoryAxis](?path=/docs/documentation-chart-components-subcomponents-axes-categoryaxis--page) pages for more information on how to configure axes.
